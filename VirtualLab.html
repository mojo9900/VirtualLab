Wind Energy Virtual Lab

Welcome to the Wind Energy Virtual Lab. You will be able to design, build and test a wind turbine. Your challenge is to create a turbine that will supply a village of 300 homes with electricity for a year at the highest efficiency and lowest cost.

Introduction

You will be able to design, build and test a wind turbine, a device that converts kinetic energy from the wind into mechanical energy. A generator connected to the turbine shaft then converts the mechanical energy into electrical energy. Your challenge is to create a turbine that will produce enough energy to supply a village of 300 homes whilst achieving highest efficiency.

Lab Goals

The target is to generate enough energy to supply at least 300 homes with renewable wind energy per year with the highest efficiency and lowest cost.
